1) VIDEO GENERATION — Wan 2.2 family (Apache-2.0)
   ComfyUI folder: models/diffusion_models/
   ----------------------------------------------------------------
   wan2.2_t2v_high_noise_14B_fp8_scaled.safetensors   ~14 GB   B-roll (text-to-video), 1st half
   wan2.2_t2v_low_noise_14B_fp8_scaled.safetensors    ~14 GB   B-roll (text-to-video), 2nd half
   wan2.2_i2v_high_noise_14B_fp8_scaled.safetensors   ~14 GB   Product (image-to-video), 1st half
   wan2.2_i2v_low_noise_14B_fp8_scaled.safetensors    ~14 GB   Product (image-to-video), 2nd half
   wan2.2_s2v_14B_fp8_scaled.safetensors              ~14 GB   Avatar (sound-to-video lipsync)

   Remember it like this: t2v = B-roll ads, i2v = Product ads,
   s2v = Avatar ads. t2v/i2v come in PAIRS (high+low noise);
   s2v is a single file.

   ComfyUI folder: models/loras/     (~0.6 GB each)
   ----------------------------------------------------------------

   wan2.2_t2v_lightx2v_4steps_lora_v1.1_low_noise.safetensors
   wan2.2_i2v_lightx2v_4steps_lora_v1_high_noise.safetensors      Product fast mode
   wan2.2_i2v_lightx2v_4steps_lora_v1_low_noise.safetensors

   Rule of thumb: LoRA ON = Preview drafts (minutes).
   LoRA OFF + 20 steps = Master quality (Avatar quality REQUIRES
   LoRA off — it visibly degrades s2v).
